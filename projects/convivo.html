01 / The premise

Beyond efficiency.

Contemporary kitchens are highly resolved as workspaces, yet often overlook the social reality around them. Convivo asks how the kitchen can support preparation without separating the person cooking from everyone else.

Brief

A domestic system in stainless steel.

Design a modern, modular indoor kitchen that leverages the durability, elegance and hygienic qualities of stainless steel in an American context. The brief focuses on ergonomic furniture, functional accessories and seamless appliance integration, enhanced by luxurious finishes and materials for practicality, low maintenance and timeless appeal.

Our vision

Precision, honesty and contemporary refinement.

We are a group of designers passionate about high-quality stainless steel. We work with the material in a way that highlights its modern design potential, strength and durability, while preserving its raw, authentic aesthetic.

Context

Gathering at home.

Gathering at home is central to American life, where family and friends come together to share meals, celebrate traditions and strengthen bonds. These gatherings reflect hospitality, connection and the creation of lasting memories through shared experiences.

Moodboard

Architecture, materiality and human rituals.

This moodboard explores the intersection of architecture, materiality, and human rituals. References emphasize calm spatial compositions, refined stainless-steel craftsmanship, modular systems, and environments that naturally encourage gathering and interaction. Warm hospitality, domestic rituals, and restrained detailing shape the project’s emotional direction, while contemporary interiors and minimalist furniture inspire a timeless aesthetic. Together, these influences establish Convivo as a kitchen that seamlessly integrates function, atmosphere, and meaningful social experiences.

02 / Context

Gathering means more.

Food is not only nourishment. Hosting at home becomes an expression of hospitality, generosity and belonging—yet the host is frequently isolated by the space designed to support it.

Gathering

Unlike purely family-centred meals, modern gatherings often include friends, neighbours and colleagues. Shared meals remain vital to social bonding.

Means more

Food is not just nourishment. It is a symbol of hospitality, warmth and connection, and hosting at home becomes a heartfelt expression of care, generosity and belonging.

Before and after COVID

Before COVID, restaurants ruled social life. Homes now offer comfort and safety, with kitchens transformed into intimate hubs for meaningful connection and shared moments.

The other side

Gatherings bring joy, but hosting is stressful. Cooking and entertaining clash, often leaving hosts alone and overwhelmed in the kitchen.

04 / Convivo

A contemporary living object.

Convivo is not just a kitchen. It goes beyond functional needs, integrating roles typically fulfilled by separate furniture into one cohesive system. By redefining boundaries, it tackles the isolation of closed-off kitchens and creates a more open, connected and inviting environment.

01 / 03

02 / 03

03 / 03

Enjoying with everyone

Why be separated when you can enjoy other people’s company without sacrificing functionality? An open central relationship keeps cooking, conversation and shared participation connected.

01

Blending

Mimicking a living space more than a conventional kitchen allows Convivo to belong to its surroundings without giving up aesthetics.

02

Adaptation

A modular kitchen gives users the option to change its arrangement according to space, activity and need.

03

Constant dialogue

Closed on both sides, Convivo can sit in the middle of a room without disturbing it. Its shape remains in dialogue with the environment while the panel can conceal preparation clutter whenever separation is needed.

05 / Two realities

A boundary that participates.

Convivo changes how much the kitchen reveals. From the living side, open shelving creates a curated display while concealing kitchen functions. From the kitchen side, integrated storage and concealed preparation areas improve workflow, cleanliness and everyday cooking.

08 / Design language

Architectural Simplicity

Soft radiused edges, clean horizontal layering and integrated architectural volumes create a calm visual identity. Every detail is restrained, balancing warmth with precision while expressing modularity, production feasibility and timeless presence.

Accessible organization

Clearer access, less movement.

Drawers replace traditional sliding cabinets because they provide faster, clearer access during cooking. Strategically placed units keep tools visible and within reach, reducing unnecessary movement.

Integrated ventilation

Performance without visual interruption.

The ventilation system is concealed within the backsplash, extracting fumes while preserving clean architectural surfaces. Removing the overhead hood improves openness, continuity and the social quality of the kitchen.

09 / Modules & configurations
