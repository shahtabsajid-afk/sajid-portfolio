01 / Context

The rituals that once brought people together.

Modern life moves at an ever-increasing pace, leaving little room for the moments that once brought people together. Yet memories of shared warmth, conversation, and presence remain deeply rooted in everyday life. KIAM begins with this emotional reality, asking how contemporary light can restore a sense of intimacy and connection within modern interiors.

Brief

Light as a reason to gather.

Develop a contemporary lighting collection inspired by the emotional experience of gathering around fire. The project explores how portable and architectural light can create atmosphere, encourage social interaction, and support moments of calm within contemporary living environments.

02 / Research

From utility to atmosphere.

The research followed layered illumination, decorative presence, social tables and portable use to understand how the intensity, warmth and position of light influence behaviour.

Layered lighting

As everyday life becomes increasingly fast-paced, moments of rest and emotional comfort become more valuable. Layered lighting combines ambient, task and accent sources to build depth, flexibility and atmosphere, allowing an interior to move naturally between practical daily use and intimate evening rituals.

Lamp as decoration

The role of a lamp extends beyond illumination. Its proportion, materiality and presence contribute to an interior even when it is switched off, allowing the object to function as a quiet architectural element rather than only a technical source.

Lamp in social reality

Lighting has long played a central role in bringing people together. Small localized sources establish a shared territory around a table, encouraging sustained attention, slower conversation and a stronger sense of intimacy.

Portable light experience

Portable lighting allows atmosphere to follow people. Freed from a fixed electrical point, it supports flexible rituals across indoor and outdoor settings while giving users direct control over the position and emotional quality of light.

03 / User research & trend

Light becomes a subtle social medium.

01

Adaptable to Gathering & Entertainment

Contemporary living environments increasingly require flexible spaces that support multiple activities throughout the day. Portable illumination contributes to this adaptability by allowing people to reshape atmosphere around relaxation, entertainment, work, and social interaction.

02

Lighting & Human Interaction

Lighting influences far more than visibility. Its intensity, warmth, and position shape how people perceive space, interact with one another, and experience emotional comfort. Soft, localized illumination naturally encourages longer conversations, focused attention, and a sense of intimacy without demanding attention itself. Rather than acting solely as a functional object, light becomes a subtle social medium that supports connection, relaxation, and meaningful everyday interactions.

03

Shared Warmth Moments

In increasingly fast-paced lives, people seek moments that restore emotional balance through simple shared experiences. Rather than constant stimulation, they value warmth, presence, and meaningful connection with family and friends. Soft lighting becomes an emotional anchor for these moments.

07 / Conceptualism & development

A form built around glow.

Kiam is inspired by the emotional experience of gathering around a fire. Across cultures and generations, shared moments have often formed around a central, warm source of light. Through a series of conceptual explorations, proportion studies, material tests, and lighting experiments, the final form creates a contemporary focal point for conversation, intimacy, and shared experience.

01

Conceptual exploration

The inverted relationship between a luminous diffuser and a grounded metal body concentrates light at the centre, creating a recognizable silhouette at two different scales.

02

3D Modelling with Rhino

Kiam was developed in Rhino through a precise surface-modelling workflow focused on proportion, symmetry, and geometric clarity. Iterative refinement balanced the relationship between the upper diffuser and tapered body while maintaining visual lightness and production feasibility.

03

Blender Rendering

Blender was used to explore materials, light behaviour, and atmosphere. Iterative studies evaluated the relationship between the reflective metal body and soft diffuser glow, allowing the emotional character of KIAM to be communicated through controlled reflections and warm ambient illumination.

04

Post-Production as a Design Tool

Post-production was used as an extension of the design process rather than a final graphic treatment. Color temperature, glow, contrast, reflections and material response were refined to create a coherent visual language that communicates warmth without spectacle.

05

Prototyping

A full-scale prototype was created to evaluate proportion, balance, light diffusion and the relationship between the suspended luminous element and outer body. Physical testing supported continuous refinement beyond what could be assessed digitally.

Quiet Presence

KIAM provides a gentle source of light for transitional outdoor moments. Its soft glow introduces emotional presence without demanding attention.

Light in Motion

Designed for portability, KIAM moves effortlessly between indoor and outdoor settings. The integrated carrying loop allows the lamp to accompany users throughout the evening, providing a reliable source of ambient light while reinforcing a sense of comfort, freedom, and flexibility.

03 / Shared ritual

Gathering Around Light

Inspired by the emotional experience of gathering around a fire, KIAM creates a warm focal point for conversation and connection. The diffused light encourages intimacy without overpowering the environment, transforming shared moments into meaningful experiences and reinforcing the lamp's role as a catalyst for togetherness.

10 / Product architecture

Resolved from the inside outward.

Internal Parts

Inspired by the emotional experience of gathering around a fire, KIAM combines a carefully engineered internal architecture with a warm and inviting lighting experience. Each component is designed to balance performance, durability, and portability. From the optical system that diffuses light softly through the glass diffuser to the rechargeable battery that enables cordless use, the layered assembly integrates thermal management, structural support, electronic control, and charging functionality within a compact form.

Dimensions

Designed to balance visual presence and portability, KIAM features a wide upper diffuser that creates a generous ambient glow while maintaining a compact overall footprint. The carefully proportioned geometry enhances light distribution, stability, and ease of handling, allowing the lamp to transition seamlessly between indoor and outdoor environments.
